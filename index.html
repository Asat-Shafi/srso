Sindh Rural Support Organization

Established in 2003, the Sindh Rural Support Organization is a not-for-profit organization registered under Section 42 of the Companies Act 2017. The SRSO is present in 15 districts. The mandate of SRSO is to alleviate poverty by harnessing the people s potential and to undertake development activities in Sindh....
